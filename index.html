Module 1: Online Banking - Introduction

Purpose: Learn to manage personal finances securely and efficiently.

Online banking allows you to manage your finances from the comfort of your home. It’s secure, convenient, and saves time. You’ll learn to set up an account, log in, and perform basic tasks.

Module 1: How to Set Up an Online Banking Account

Step 1: Visit your bank's website or download its mobile app.
Step 2: Locate the “Sign Up” or “Register” button.
Step 3: Fill out the form with your personal details, such as your name, account number, and a secure password.
Why It Matters: Registering ensures your account is protected, and you can access it anytime securely.
