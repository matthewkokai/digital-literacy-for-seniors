Module 3: Social Media Use - Introduction

Purpose: Learn how to safely and effectively engage with social media platforms.

Social media allows you to connect with friends and family, join communities, and stay informed. This module will teach you the basics of using social media safely.

Module 3: Setting Up a Social Media Account

Step 1: Choose a platform, such as Facebook or Instagram.
Step 2: Visit the platform’s website or download its app.
Step 3: Fill out the registration form with your name, email, and a secure password.
Why It Matters: Social media accounts allow you to safely engage with content and connect with others.

Module 4: Cybersecurity Basics - Introduction

Purpose: Protect yourself online by identifying threats and adopting secure practices.

Cybersecurity is crucial for keeping your information safe. This module will teach you how to identify risks and implement security measures.

Module 4: Identifying Online Threats

Step 1: Be cautious of emails asking for personal information.
Step 2: Avoid clicking on suspicious links.
Step 3: Use antivirus software to protect your device.
Why It Matters: Recognizing threats can prevent identity theft and data breaches.
