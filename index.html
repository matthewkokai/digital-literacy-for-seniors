Module 1: Online Banking - Overview

What is Online Banking? Online banking allows you to manage your finances through your bank's website or app without visiting a branch.

Key Benefits:
  • 24/7 access to your account.
  • Pay bills and transfer funds quickly.
  • Track transactions and manage your budget.

Module 1: How to Set Up Online Banking

Follow these steps to set up an online banking account:

Step 1: Visit your bank's website or download their app.
Step 2: Register with your account information and create a secure password.
Step 3: Set up security features such as two-factor authentication for added protection.

Module 2: Video Calling - Overview

What is Video Calling? Video calling allows you to connect with friends, family, and colleagues visually and audibly over the internet.

Why Use Video Calling?
  • Stay connected with loved ones regardless of distance.
  • Participate in virtual meetings from the comfort of your home.
  • Save time and costs associated with travel.

Module 2: Setting Up Video Calls

Follow these steps to set up a video call:

Step 1: Download a video calling app such as Zoom or Skype.
Step 2: Create an account or sign in with existing credentials.
Step 3: Schedule or start a new meeting and invite participants.
