Empowering Seniors: Mastering Digital Literacy

Welcome to the Digital Literacy Course

This course is designed to help seniors master essential digital skills, including:

  • Online Banking
  • Video Calling
  • Social Media
  • Cybersecurity

Module 1: Online Banking - Introduction

Purpose: Learn to manage personal finances securely and efficiently.

This module will guide you through setting up an account, navigating your bank’s website, and completing basic tasks like checking balances and paying bills.

Module 1: Setting Up Your Account

Step 1: Open your web browser and navigate to your bank's website.
Step 2: Locate the "Sign In" or "Log In" button and click it.
Step 3: Enter your username and password. If you don’t have an account, click on "Create Account" and follow the steps to register.

Module 2: Video Calling - Introduction

Purpose: Connect with family, friends, and professionals through video calls.

This module will guide you through installing a video calling app, creating an account, and practicing key features like joining and hosting meetings.

Module 2: Setting Up the App

Step 1: Download and install a video calling app like Zoom or Skype from your app store.
Step 2: Open the app and create an account using your email address.
